With over 30 years of experience under its belt, Indraprashta Apollo Hospital has become the favored destination of millions of patients spanning across different countries. Fortis Heart Institute

Fortis Hospital is one of the top known hospital of Mulund area. It provides multi-specialty service to its patients around the city as well as those that visit it from across India and also globally. It has highly skilled and experienced surgeons and paramedical staff. Areas of multi-specialty include cardiology, urology, cardiac surgery, nephrology, neurosciences, orthopedics, etc.

Artemis Hospital,Gurgaon

Artemis Hospital is a state-of-art multi-specialty hospital located in Gurgaon, Haryana. This hospital is the first to achieve JCI and NABH Accreditation in Gurgaon. It is one of the most advanced hospitals in India. Within a short span of its launch in the year 2007, Artemis hospital has gained global recognition; thanks to the depth of medical expertise it possesses.
