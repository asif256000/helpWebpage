Lilavati Hospital

Lilavati Hospital & Research Centre was conceptualized and brought into reality by Late Shri Kirtilal Mehta in loving memory of his wife, Smt. Lilavati Kirtilal Mehta. It is a prestigious and acclaimed multi-specialty hospital in Mumbai run by the Lilavati Kirtilal Mehta Medical Trust.

Fortis Hospital Mulund

Fortis Hospital is one of the top known hospital of Mulund area. It provides multi-specialty service to its patients around the city as well as those that visit it from across India and also globally. It has highly skilled and experienced surgeons and paramedical staff. Areas of multi-specialty include cardiology, urology, cardiac surgery, nephrology, neurosciences, orthopedics, etc.

K.D. Ambani Hospital

K.D. Ambani Hospital (KDAH) is a fine example of how compassion, dedication, and ethical principles can achieve excellence in the medical world. Initiated by a prominent personality of the Indian medical field, Dr. Nitu Mandke, and with the help of financial and management support from Dhirubai Ambani.

S.L Raheja Hospital

Considered as a Center of Excellence with regards to Oncology and Diabetes, the S.L Raheja Hospital in Mumbai also provides tertiary treatment and care for all major specialties. The hospital primarily focuses on prevention via early diagnosis. With over two decades of dedicated health care, the hospital has gained a good reputation for provision of high class service and quality medical aid.
